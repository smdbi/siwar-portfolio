DMS — Monte Carlo & DORA

We forecast delivery windows by combining recent throughput with DORA metrics to produce P80/P95 UAT ranges.

  • Role: Business Analyst · QA
  • Domain: DMS · SaaS
  • Stack: Python, Jupyter, Jira/Azure Boards
  • Timeline: 6 weeks

On this page

  1. Overview
  2. Problem & Goals
  3. Approach
  4. Outcomes
  5. Tech & Methods
  6. Lessons

Overview

  • Defined acceptance criteria tied to DORA signals.
  • Used historical throughput to simulate likely completion dates.
  • Published P80/P95 ranges with confidence notes in release docs.

Problem & Goals

Stakeholders needed reliable date ranges instead of single bets. Confidence needed to be evidence-based and explained simply.

Approach

  1. Extracted weekly throughput from boards.
  2. Ran 10k Monte Carlo simulations for delivery windows.
  3. Communicated P80/P95 with visual bands and plain-language notes.

Outcomes

  • P80UAT range within plan
  • ClearStakeholder expectations
  • FewerScope churn debates

Tech & Methods

  • Monte Carlo
  • DORA
  • Python
  • Analytics

Lessons

  • Ranges + assumptions > single target date.
